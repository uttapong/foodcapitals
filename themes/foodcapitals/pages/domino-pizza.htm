Domino’s Pizza

Domino's Pizza, founded in 1960 and based in Ann Arbor, Michigan, United States, is the second largest pizza chain in the United States and also the second-largest in the world.  Domino is best known for delivery service, offering vegetarian and meat pizzas, fried chicken, sandwiches, pasta, bread, and a variety of dessert. Today, there are more than 8,000 stores worldwide
